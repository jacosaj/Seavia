About

Learn More About Our Mission

At Seavia, we specialise in refurbishment of interiors for the marine industry. Our strength is that we are flexible and able to adapt to changes that may arise during the project so we always care about the satisfaction of our clients.

We provide construction and renovation of marine interiors.

Including restaurants, store areas, public spaces, casinos, cafes, pubs, crew and passenger cabins.

  • Quality is our priority.

    We pay attention to details and work easily with innovative designs and materials.

  • Our team, which has worked on over 80 projects,

    has gained experience in the construction of new and conversion of older ships, willingly taking on challenges that require initiative when working with non- standard projects.

We take pride in guaranteeing our work and have the capacity to perform the most difficult and the most demanding projects commissioned by the constructors and designers.
